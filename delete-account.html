Delete Account - ChefAi

At ChefAi, we respect your right to control your personal data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, you can easily do so by following the instructions below.

How to delete your account

Option 1: From the ChefAi app (Recommended)

Steps to follow:

  1. Open the ChefAi application on your device
  2. Go to the settings menu (profile icon with wrench in the top left corner)
  3. Find and tap "Delete account"
  4. Read the warning about permanent deletion
  5. Confirm deletion by tapping "Delete forever"

This is the fastest and most direct way to delete your account.

Important Warning

Account deletion is permanent and irreversible.

Once you confirm deletion, we cannot recover any data. Make sure you really want to proceed before confirming.

What data gets deleted?

Data deleted immediately:

  • Profile information: Name, age, email, dietary preferences
  • Pantry data: All ingredients and expiration dates
  • Recipes: All generated recipes and those marked as favorites
  • Settings: Language, application preferences
  • Usage history: Usage statistics and activity
  • Photos: Any processed ingredient images

Data retention information

Immediate deletion (0 days)

All personal data stored on our servers is deleted immediately when you confirm account deletion. There is no grace period or temporary retention.

Billing data (Stripe)

Payment data is processed by Stripe (our payment provider). This data:

  • Is not stored on our ChefAi servers
  • Is managed directly by Stripe according to their policies
  • Does not contain ChefAi personal information

To delete Stripe data, you must contact their customer service directly.

AI data (OpenAI)

Data sent to OpenAI for recipe generation:

  • Is processed anonymously (without personal identifiers)
  • Is not permanently stored by OpenAI according to their policies
  • Is automatically deleted after processing

After deleting your account

What happens immediately:

  • You will not be able to access your ChefAi account
  • All your recipes and data will have been permanently deleted
  • Your Premium subscription will be automatically canceled
  • You will not receive any more communications from ChefAi

If you change your mind

After deleting your account, if you want to use ChefAi again:

  • You will have to create a new account from scratch
  • We cannot recover any previous data
  • You will have to set up your profile again
  • You will lose all previous favorite recipes

Alternatives to deleting your account

Before deleting, consider:

  • Uninstall the app: You can simply uninstall without deleting data
  • Cancel Premium: You can use the free version
  • Clear data: You can delete recipes individually
  • Change settings: Adjust privacy preferences
